    • WHO WE ARE

    Amici Servicesis a independent service company specializing in the sale and maintenance of Espresso machines, coffee machines and grinders. In fact we like to say “If coffee runs through it we do it”. With 15 years experience in the coffee service industry we decided to start our own business and 4 years later we continue to grow. We recently moved from our home office/shop to a new easily accessible, centrally located Business Park in Parkrose which allows us the space to grow for many years to come.

     

    Why We're Different

    Because We Care! We have a complete showroom where we have equipment ready to tryout. We offer a hands on experience to use and observe what the new equipment capabilities are. We can also offer the services of a professional barista for training. (picture of show room with steph in front of machine.) Our knowledge and years of experience are key to our ability to provide exceptional service on Rancilio, Nuova Simoneli, Faema, La San Marco, La Marzocco, La Spazial, Reneka, Rio- Astoria espresso machines and more. We also have expert knowledge on the original ECM line of espresso machines, having worked directly with the manufacturer on improvements to create an optimum performance in an espresso machine. Coffee brewers such as Bunn, Fetco, Bloomfield, and Curtis are equipment we routinely service, along with the following Grinders, Mazzer Macap, Obel, Rossi, Compak, Swift, Bunn, Grindmaster and Mahlkoenig.

    Resources for owners of all of our fine products
