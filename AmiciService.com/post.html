    BE KINDER TO YOUR GRINDER

    AUTHOR: VIC TUTTLE // CATEGORY: GRINDERS: MAINTENANCE, CARE, AND UPKEEP

    0 COMMENTS

    Be kinder to your grinder. Grinders are one of critical components of your coffee business. A weekly cleaning is recommended as well as regular replacement of grinding burrs. Have you ever walked into a coffee shop and heard those espresso grinders screaming? Not only is that annoying but more importantly is indicates the burrs are shot. This also makes you grinder run longer and can overheat your grinder. If you are grinding 50 to 75 Pounds per month, you should be replacing your burrs every 6 months.

    Dirty hoppers stained with rancid coffee oils can not only be a visual turn off for you customers but this rancid oil can taint the fresh beans we all enjoy. Just a simple replacement of a broken doser lever return spring can make a baristas life easier.
